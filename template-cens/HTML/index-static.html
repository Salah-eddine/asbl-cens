Welcome!

We are fully approved by the Horse Society as a professional riding school

Our modern and fully equipped equestrian centre is happy to offer everything from polo and show-jumping to horse riding trips across the hills and mountains. Energise your mind, body and soul with a visit to our spacious & accommodating center. At our centre you will acquire new riding skills or simply will have a chance to relax in a good atmosphere.

Read more about us

01

Riding Lessons

Sunset Riding, Beach Rides, Pony Rides

We offer a broad range of riding services, from child or adult novices to advanced dressage and jumping programs.

02

Horse Breeding

Pet, Groom, Feed

We have a variety of services for horse breeders. They include affordable grooming and feeding for your horses.

03

Other Services

Gift Certificates, Weddings, Photoshoots

If you are a real fan of horses, then you should take a look at our additional services, which also cover special events organization.
